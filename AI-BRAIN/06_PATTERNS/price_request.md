# Pattern: Price Request

## Signal

- "quanto custa"
- "qual o preço"
- "tem promoção"

## Action

1. Ask which product the lead is interested in
2. Suggest available options from entity list

## Error

- Do not answer price directly without knowing the product
- Do not quote price without collecting CEP first
